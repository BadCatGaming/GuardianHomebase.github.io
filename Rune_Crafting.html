Rune Crafting is creating magically imbued Runes for many purposes. They can be imbued into weapons to increase durability or become wreathed in flames. Runes can create armor that will deflect projectiles back at attackers or heal minor wounds. Runes are also used to create magic charges for traps and cheaper casting. Rune smiths work hand in hand with blacksmiths most of the time. And many blacksmiths also work as Rune smiths
